Welcome to KBase

After signing in, you can start working with KBase. Upload your experimental data and perform comparative genomics and systems biology analyses by creating Narratives: interactive, dynamic, and shareable documents. Narratives include all your analysis steps, commentary, and visualizations.

Want to learn more? Check out the Narrative Interface User Guide or the Narrative Interface video tutorial, and a library of tutorials that show you how to use various KBase apps to analyze your data.
